## 🤖 AI & Technology

### Colorado AI Law Takes Effect Today
**Colorado's Consumer Protections for Artificial Intelligence Act** officially takes effect today (June 30). It applies to developers and deployers of high-risk AI systems covering employment, healthcare, financial services, education, housing, and legal services. Requirements include a risk management program, annual impact assessments, disclosure obligations, and the right for affected residents to appeal AI-driven decisions.

### OpenAI's First Custom Chip
OpenAI revealed its **first custom AI chip** — a significant step toward reducing dependence on Nvidia. Details on the chip's specs and deployment timeline are still emerging.

### Anthropic vs. Alibaba
Anthropic formally accused Alibaba of running **28.8 million fraudulent exchanges** against Claude, in what appears to be a large-scale attempt to extract model behavior or circumvent usage policies.

### Gemini 3.5 Pro Still AWOL
Google's Gemini 3.5 Pro **missed Sundar Pichai's "give us until next month" commitment** from Google I/O on May 19. It remains limited-preview only. Meanwhile, Gemini 2.5 Pro with Deep Think (launched June 22) is posting strong reasoning benchmark scores: 82.4% GPQA Diamond, 89.8% MMLU-Pro.

### Apple iOS 27 Goes All-In on AI
Apple is embedding AI throughout **iOS 27** — automatic restaurant bill splitting, AI-assisted password replacement after breaches, intelligent message suggestions, and more everyday automation features.

### Google Restricts Meta's AI Access
Google has restricted Meta's use of Gemini models after Meta requested more computing capacity than Google could supply. Google also reportedly signed a deal to rent additional cloud capacity from **Elon Musk's SpaceX** to ease the bottleneck.

### AI Job Cuts Hit Record High
A new analysis found **AI was behind 88,000 US job cuts in 2026** — more than all prior years combined.

### Infrastructure & Investment
- **Berkshire Hathaway made a $10 billion private placement in Alphabet** — one of the largest single tech investments the conglomerate has ever made. Alphabet's 2026 capex guidance: $180–190 billion.
- **Comcast officially announced a tax-free spin-off**, separating its media business from its broadband/wireless/connectivity operations serving 65 million US households.

### Cybersecurity
Japanese telecom **KDDI disclosed a breach** affecting up to **14.2 million email logins**. Separately, security researchers flagged an exploited flaw in Lantronix serial-to-IP converters used in industrial systems.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Record Heat: 41.1°C in Doksany
The **Czech Hydrometeorological Institute confirmed a new national temperature record** — 41.1°C at Doksany on Sunday, the first time an official Czech weather station has recorded above 41°C. The weekend heatwave caused **85 collapses in Prague alone**. A cold front with storms and hail is expected to arrive soon.

### Czech Casting Trafficking Case
A Prague prosecutor charged **nine people** in connection with **Czech Casting** — a pornography operation that allegedly lured young women with false promises of legitimate modelling work between 2016 and 2019. The indictment covers alleged offences against 18 women.

### Keanu Reeves Visits Prague Castle
Actor **Keanu Reeves met President Petr Pavel** and reportedly shared beers at the Prague Castle pub during a visit to the Czech capital.

### Energy Drink Ban Proposed for Under-16s
A proposed ban on energy drink sales to under-16s is gaining traction following a surge in energy drink consumption among teenagers.

## 🥊 MMA / UFC

### UFC Fight Night: Fiziev vs. Torres (June 27, Azerbaijan)
The latest Fight Night card was held at the **National Gymnastics Arena in Baku**. The main event was a lightweight bout between **#3 Rafael Fiziev** (Azerbaijan) and **#15 Manuel Torres** (Mexico). Full results still coming in.

### Paramount+ Becomes Exclusive UFC Home
Starting 2026, **Paramount+ will exclusively stream all UFC events for 7 years** — ending the traditional PPV model. All events now stream at no extra cost to Paramount+ subscribers.

### Bigger Bonuses
From UFC 324 onwards, **Performance of the Night and Fight of the Night bonuses doubled** from $50,000 to **$100,000**.

### Meta UFC Rankings Replace Media Voting
The UFC replaced its weekly media voting panel with the **Meta UFC Rankings** — an Elo-based mathematical model that factors in opponent quality, activity level, and decay for long layoffs.
