## 🤖 AI & Technology

### Anthropic Overtakes OpenAI in Revenue
Anthropic crossed **$30 billion in annualized revenue** in April 2026, surpassing OpenAI's $25B ARR for the first time — a 30x growth in just 15 months, the fastest expansion in enterprise software history. Anthropic's revenue is ~80% enterprise, versus OpenAI's more consumer-heavy mix (ChatGPT subscriptions). The company also signed one of the **largest compute deals ever disclosed** to support continued scaling.

### ChatGPT at 1 Billion Users — but Losing Share
ChatGPT crossed the symbolic **1 billion monthly active users** milestone, but its global web traffic share has dropped to **54.7%** (down from 76.5% in Feb 2025). Google Gemini surged to second place at ~27.4%.

### AI Regulation Countdown
- **Colorado AI Act** takes effect **June 30** — covering high-risk AI in employment, healthcare, financial services, education, housing, and legal contexts.
- **EU AI Act** requirements for high-risk AI systems go live **August 2**, including law enforcement, critical infrastructure, and education.

### Apple Prepares iPhone Price Hikes
Apple is readying price increases for upcoming iPhone models as **AI-driven memory shortages** ripple through the supply chain, affecting DRAM and NAND flash availability worldwide.

### Ransomware Hits Education Sector
Cybercriminals are accelerating attacks on ed-tech platforms. A June 2026 attack on **GSF** disrupted classes for tens of thousands of students — part of a broader wave targeting schools and learning platforms.

## 🏀 NBA

### 🏆 Knicks Are NBA Champions!

The **New York Knicks** ended a 53-year drought, defeating the San Antonio Spurs **94–90 in Game 5** to win their third NBA championship.

| Game | Winner | Score |

**Game 5 hero:** Jalen Brunson scored **45 points**, including **13 consecutive points in the 4th quarter**, and was named **Finals MVP**. The Knicks trailed by 16 at halftime — their fourth double-digit deficit overcome in the series.

**Wembanyama:** Had a brilliant series finale with 19 pts, 14 reb, and 5 blocks, but wasn't enough. Dylan Harper led the Spurs with 25 pts.

## 🥊 MMA / UFC

### Gaethje Shocks the World at the White House (June 14)
At **UFC Freedom 250** on the South Lawn of the White House — the first sporting event ever held there — **Justin Gaethje** pulled off a massive upset, stopping champion **Ilia Topuria** via **TKO (corner stoppage) at the end of Round 4**.
