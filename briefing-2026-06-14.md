## 🤖 Artificial Intelligence

### ChatGPT Hits 1 Billion Monthly Active Users
OpenAI's ChatGPT has crossed the symbolic milestone of **1 billion monthly active users**, cementing its lead in the AI assistant market at **54.7% of worldwide web visits** across the top 7 chatbots. Google Gemini is second at 27.4%, up ~104% over the past six months.

### NVIDIA Releases Nemotron 3 Ultra (550B)
NVIDIA announced **Nemotron 3 Ultra**, a 550-billion parameter open-weights model — the largest and most capable US open-weights model to date.

### US AI Executive Order Signed
The President signed an Executive Order titled **"Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security"** (June 2). AI companies are asked to voluntarily provide frontier models to the federal government up to 30 days before public release for cybersecurity benchmarking.

### Colorado AI Law Takes Effect June 30
Colorado's **Consumer Protections for Artificial Intelligence Act** — applying to deployers and developers of high-risk AI systems serving Colorado residents — goes live on June 30, 2026.

## 💻 Technology

### SpaceX IPO — Largest in History
SpaceX priced its IPO at **$135/share**, raising ~$75 billion in what became the **largest IPO in history**, valuing the company at ~$1.77 trillion. It listed on Nasdaq under ticker **SPCX** on June 13.

### Google Gemini M5 Update
A major update to Google's Gemini, codenamed **"M5"**, rolled out featuring enhanced NPU acceleration and a 128B parameter architecture.

### Jeff Bezos Bets $12B on "Artificial General Engineer"
Bezos is backing a $12B bet on a system described as an **"artificial general engineer"** — AI designed to autonomously redesign everything from jet engines to medicines.

### AI Infrastructure Race Heats Up
The global AI race in 2026 is increasingly an **infrastructure and energy race** — China is making a massive AI infrastructure push while US regulators tighten oversight. Data center backlash is growing. Apple is reported to be resetting Siri's AI strategy.

### Quantum Computing Milestone
Quantum computing took a **major leap toward real-world accessibility**, though specific technical details are still emerging.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Czech Army Recruitment on Track
The Czech Army recruited ~**1,400 new soldiers** in the first five months of 2026, on track to exceed its annual target. The military now has 25,000 professional soldiers and 5,500 active reservists.

### Ukrainian Refugees — EU Backs Czech Plan
The EU endorsed a Czech proposal to extend refugee protection **with limits for military-age men**. Czechia hosts the most Ukrainian refugees per capita in the EU — over **398,000 registered** as of February 2026, with ~6,000 arriving monthly.

### Czech Television Strike Warning
Czech Television staff plan a **warning strike on June 22** over proposed changes to state budget financing.

### Unemployment Slightly Down
Czech unemployment dropped slightly to **4.8%**, though it remains historically high for the month of May.

### Two Czech Priests Beatified in Brno
Two Czech priests executed by communists were **beatified before 13,000 people** in Brno — a significant moment for the Czech Catholic community.

### Karlovy Vary Film Festival
**Maggie Gyllenhaal** and **Jesse Eisenberg** will receive the Karlovy Vary International Film Festival President's Awards at this year's edition.

## 🏀 NBA

### 🏆 NEW YORK KNICKS WIN THE 2026 NBA CHAMPIONSHIP!
In the biggest story of the night — the **New York Knicks defeated the San Antonio Spurs 94–90 in Game 5** to claim the franchise's **first NBA title since 1973** (53 years).

- **Jalen Brunson** was unstoppable, dropping **45 points** including 13 straight in the 4th quarter.
- The Knicks won the series **4–1**, erasing a double-digit deficit in all four victories — including a 16-point comeback in the clincher.
- Every game of the series was within 5 points in the final 5 minutes — the most clutch Finals in 30 years.
- **Brunson won Finals MVP.**
