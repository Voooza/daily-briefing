## 🤖 AI & Technology

### Anthropic Export Controls Still in Effect
The US government's **June 12 export control directive** keeps Anthropic's **Fable 5 and Mythos 5** offline for foreign nationals. Canadian PM Mark Carney cited the restrictions as evidence countries should avoid over-relying on a limited number of AI providers.

### Trump Signs Revised AI & Cybersecurity Executive Order
President Trump signed an expanded executive order on **AI Innovation and Security** directing federal agencies to strengthen cybersecurity capabilities, establish a cybersecurity clearinghouse, and develop a **classified benchmarking process** for advanced AI models' cyber capabilities.

### OpenAI Partner Network Launches
OpenAI launched its formal **OpenAI Partner Network** for consultants, integrators, and tech providers — targeting certification of **300,000 consultants** by end of 2026.

### Google Releases Gemini 2.5 Pro with Deep Think
Google shipped **Gemini 2.5 Pro** featuring a new **Deep Think reasoning mode**, described as their most capable model to date.

### ChatGPT Passes 1 Billion Monthly Active Users
ChatGPT now has **1 billion monthly active users**, holding ~54.7% of worldwide AI chatbot traffic. Google Gemini is climbing fast at 27.4% — up ~104% in six months.

### Meta Building AI Wearables
Meta is developing an **AI-powered pendant** and a business product called **"Wearables for Work"**, while expanding its AI glasses line.

### Nvidia RTX Spark Superchip
Nvidia unveiled the **RTX Spark Superchip** for laptops and mini-PCs — combining Blackwell RTX graphics with Grace CPU, bringing full local AI processing to consumer hardware.

## 💻 Tech Industry

### SpaceX $75 Billion IPO + Acquires Cursor
SpaceX is moving toward a **$75 billion IPO** to fund space-based AI data centers. Separately, SpaceX announced plans to acquire **Anysphere** (parent of AI coding tool Cursor) in an all-stock deal valued at **$60 billion**, expected to close in Q3 2026.

### DeepSeek Raises $7.4 Billion
China's **DeepSeek** is preparing to raise **$7.4 billion** in its first funding round at a valuation of up to **$59 billion**.

### Fox Acquires Roku for $22 Billion
Fox agreed to acquire **Roku** in a $22 billion cash-and-stock deal, combining Fox Sports/entertainment, Tubi streaming, and Roku's connected TV platform.

### US Data Center Buildout Slipping
30–50% of ~140 planned US data centers targeting 16 GW may miss 2026 timelines or be canceled, with multi-year waits for transformers, grid connections, and growing local opposition over energy and water usage.

### Quantum Computing Executive Orders
President Trump signed two EOs on quantum computing aimed at protecting US cryptographic infrastructure while encouraging commercial quantum innovation.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Babiš Leads NATO Summit Delegation — Without Pavel
The government decided **not to include President Petr Pavel** in the Czech delegation to the NATO summit in Ankara on July 7–8. PM Andrej Babiš will lead, accompanied by Foreign Minister Petr Macinka and Defense Minister Jaromír Zůna.

### Public Media 24-Hour Strike
Staff at **Czech Television and Czech Radio** staged a 24-hour warning strike protesting a government proposal to transfer their funding to the state budget. Unions warned further action is likely if the government doesn't withdraw the proposal.

### Czech AI Gigafactory Approved
The cabinet approved support for Czechia's bid to host a **European AI gigafactory** — ~CZK 100 billion in total investment, operated by **České Radiokomunikace**, planned for **Zbraslav in Prague**, with an initial state commitment of CZK 2.5 billion.

### Heat Wave Incoming
Temperatures hit 31°C this week and are forecast to reach **34°C Thursday** and potentially exceed **35°C (supertropical)** by the weekend.

### Czech Tennis Double-Up
**Linda Nosková** entered the **world top 10** after winning in Berlin. **Marie Bouzková** won in Nottingham — both claiming their **first grass titles** one week before Wimbledon.
