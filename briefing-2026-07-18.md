## 🤖 AI & Technology

### China Launches Rival AI Governance Bloc: WAICO
At the opening of the World AI Conference in Shanghai, President **Xi Jinping** announced the creation of the **World Artificial Intelligence Cooperation Organisation (WAICO)**, headquartered in Shanghai, with **29 founding member countries** including Indonesia, Brazil, Malaysia, South Africa, Senegal, Russia, and Pakistan. UN Secretary-General **António Guterres** attended the signing. Xi called it "a milestone in the history of world AI development" and pitched it as a voice for the Global South in AI governance — positioning it as a rival to the US-led "Pax Silica" initiative, which has 35 signatory countries. The WAIC conference itself debuted roughly 300 new AI products.

### Germany: AI Overviews and Perplexity Are "Publishers"
Germany's media regulator ruled that **Google's AI Overviews** and **Perplexity AI** fall under German media law because their AI-generated summaries constitute original content creation — a regulatory first that could force structural changes to how AI search summaries operate in the EU.

### Efficient Open-Weight Model: Bonsai 27B
**PrismML** open-sourced **Bonsai**, a 27-billion-parameter model that fits in just **3.9 GB**, small enough to run fully offline on a phone — part of a broader trend toward private, cloud-independent AI deployment.

### Anthropic Nearing Mega-IPO
Bankers are reportedly lining up investor meetings as **Anthropic** moves closer to a large public offering.

### Also Notable
- **Alibaba** shares rose ~4% on reports that Qwen AI is set to be integrated into Apple Intelligence.
- **SpaceX** stock fell below its $135 IPO price for the first time.
- India is expanding **facial recognition surveillance** (supplied by Spain's Herta Security) across Delhi and Jaipur to monitor millions of citizens.
- Wall Street's **DTCC** is piloting tokenized markets with major industry players.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Severe Storm Warning Nationwide
The Czech Hydrometeorological Institute (**ČHMÚ**) issued a warning covering the **entire country** from Friday 13:00 through Saturday midnight (July 17–18), citing wind gusts up to **70 km/h**, up to **40 mm** of rain in short bursts, and large hail. A weak **supercell** already brought flash flooding and heavy hail over southern Bohemia and Moravia Friday evening; authorities are warning of lightning strike risk and falling branches through the weekend.

### Government Marks Six Months, 195 of 978 Pledges Done
PM **Andrej Babiš** convened his cabinet at Kramářova vila to review the coalition's (ANO, SPD, Motoristé sobě) first half-year in office: **195 of 978** program commitments fully delivered, **774** in progress, and only **9** not yet started. The next full cabinet session is scheduled for **Monday, July 20** at Strakova akademie.

### Fallout Continues from NATO Ankara "Two Delegations" Story
Coverage continues of PM Babiš and President Pavel attending the NATO summit separately after Foreign Minister Petr Macinka's dispute over Pavel's role — with Babiš having confirmed Czechia will sit out the €70bn allied Ukraine aid package, even as new NATO figures show Czechia hitting its 2.01% GDP defense-spending target this year.
