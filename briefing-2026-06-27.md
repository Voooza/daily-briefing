## 🤖 AI & Technology

### White House Signs AI Innovation & Security Executive Order
President Trump signed a new **Executive Order on Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security**, positioning the US as the global AI leader. The order explicitly blocks mandatory government licensing of AI models while establishing new security evaluation frameworks.

### Anthropic's Mythos Finds Government System Vulnerabilities
As part of **Project Glasswing** — a joint initiative with US intelligence agencies — Anthropic's **Mythos model identified critical vulnerabilities in sensitive government computer systems within hours** of testing. The initiative is designed to assess cybersecurity risks posed by advanced AI and harden critical infrastructure before broader AI deployment.

### Trump Admin Restricts GPT 5.6 Rollout
The White House has asked OpenAI to **limit the initial rollout of GPT 5.6** to a select group of trusted partners, with government agencies approving access on a customer-by-customer basis. OpenAI CEO Sam Altman notified staff after discussions with the Office of the National Cyber Director.

### OpenAI Advertising Goes Core — 900M Weekly Users
ChatGPT now serves **more than 900 million weekly active users**, with roughly one-fifth of queries expressing commercial intent. OpenAI confirmed advertising is now a **core part of its business strategy**, focusing on sponsored experiences built around usefulness rather than traditional attention metrics.

### Industry Releases Agentic Resource Discovery Spec
**Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Hugging Face, NVIDIA, Salesforce, Snowflake** and others jointly released **Agentic Resource Discovery (ARD)** — an open specification that lets AI agents locate, verify, and connect with tools, APIs, MCP servers, and other agents at runtime. A significant interoperability step for the agentic AI ecosystem.

### Amazon Enters Conversational Advertising
Amazon unveiled **Alexa+ Agentic Ads**, a conversational ad format that lets consumers ask questions, get personalized responses, and **complete purchases without leaving the ad**. A sign of where AI-native commerce is heading.

### Samsung Plans $648B South Korea Investment
Samsung Group is set to announce a **decade-long investment of 1,000 trillion won (~$648B)** in South Korea, covering chip factories, AI data centres, batteries, and displays. One of the largest single corporate investment commitments ever announced.

### iOS 27 AI Features Expand
Apple is embedding AI throughout **iOS 27** with practical features: AI-assisted bill splitting, automatic password replacement after breaches, intelligent message suggestions, natural-language calendar creation, smarter Shortcuts, contextual info during customer service calls, and AI-organised Safari tabs.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### School Year Ends — Summer Holidays Start Today
More than **1.5 million pupils and students** received end-of-year reports on June 26, with summer holidays officially starting **today, June 27** — four days ahead of the original schedule after Education Minister Robert Plaga moved the date forward. Around **39,560 Ukrainian refugee children** in Czech schools also received reports.

### Extreme Heat Warning — All-Time Record Could Fall
The **Czech Hydrometeorological Institute** warns that the all-time temperature record of **40.4 °C (set in 2012) could be broken this weekend**. Warnings are in effect across Bohemia, Olomouc, and southern Moravia. Wildfire risk is elevated due to hot, dry conditions — fires could start and spread easily.

### Czech USAR Team Deployed to Venezuela
Czechia dispatched its **urban search-and-rescue team (USAR)** to Venezuela following deadly earthquakes, with the team expected on the ground as of June 26.

### Patrik Schick Retires from International Football
Striker **Patrik Schick announced his retirement from the Czech national team** just one day after the Czechs were eliminated from the **2026 FIFA World Cup**. The end of an era for Czech football.

## 🥊 MMA / UFC

### UFC Baku: Fiziev vs. Torres — TODAY
**UFC Fight Night: Fiziev vs. Torres** takes place **today, June 27**, at the National Gymnastics Arena in **Baku, Azerbaijan**. Main card starts at 12 PM ET / 6 PM CET. The lightweight main event features **#11 Rafael Fiziev** (Azerbaijan) vs. **#15 Manuel Torres** (Mexico). A must-watch for lightweight title picture implications.

### Recap: Gaethje Defeats Topuria at UFC Freedom 250
At the historic **UFC White House event** (June 14), **Justin Gaethje defeated Ilia Topuria** in the main event, streamed live from the South Lawn of the White House on Paramount+. First professional sporting event ever held at the presidential residence.

### UFC Switches to Data-Driven Meta Rankings
As of June 22, the UFC has begun transitioning its official rankings to **Meta UFC Rankings** — an objective, data-driven system based entirely on fight performance data, replacing the traditional media-panel vote.

### No More PPV — Paramount+ Takes Over
**Paramount+** will exclusively stream all UFC events for **seven years**, with all events included in the subscription at no additional cost. The era of UFC pay-per-view is effectively over.

The **113th Tour de France** starts **July 4 in Barcelona** and runs until July 26 in Paris. At 3,320 km over 21 stages, with just 26 km of individual time trial, it's a climbers' race.

**Key contenders:**
