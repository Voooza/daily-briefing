## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Pavel Vetoes Budget-Rules Amendment
President **Petr Pavel** vetoed a government amendment to public-budget laws on July 22, warning it threatens fiscal stability. The bill would have let the government **exceed approved state budget spending by up to 10%** if the security situation worsens, and extended until **2036** the ability to raise defense spending above the 2%-of-GDP threshold without counting it against approved budget frameworks. PM **Andrej Babiš** called the veto "irresponsible" and says the coalition — which holds 108 of 200 seats, above the 101 needed — intends to override it.
