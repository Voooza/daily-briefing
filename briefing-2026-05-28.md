# Daily Briefing — May 28, 2026

---

## World News

### Middle East: Iran-Israel Conflict & US Ceasefire Tensions
Israel killed 31 people in Lebanon overnight, prompting Tehran to blast the US over what it calls a "grave violation" of the fragile ceasefire. President Trump is set to meet his Cabinet at a critical juncture in talks aimed at ending the war with Iran, with the emerging deal already drawing fierce criticism domestically. Iran has stated it is committed to restoring commercial traffic through the Strait of Hormuz to pre-war levels within one month.

### US Politics: Texas Senate Runoff
Trump-backed Ken Paxton defeated incumbent Sen. John Cornyn in the Texas Republican Senate runoff. Paxton will now face Democrat James Talarico in the general election. Separately, a House Democrat who repeatedly sought to impeach Trump was also ousted in a Texas primary race.

### Biden vs. DOJ
Former President Biden has filed suit against the Justice Department to block release of audio recordings from special counsel Robert Hur's classified documents investigation.

### Alabama Redistricting Blocked
A federal judge blocked Alabama's redistricting plan in a ruling seen as a blow to the Trump administration's priorities.

### Washington Paper Mill Explosion
At least one person was killed and nine remain missing after a chemical tank imploded at a paper mill in Washington state.

### Space: China's Shenzhou 23
China launched the Shenzhou 23 spacecraft carrying three astronauts to its space station. One astronaut is scheduled to remain in space for a full year to study human adaptability during long-duration spaceflight.

### Climate: Record Heat in Europe
London recorded 95°F (35°C) for two consecutive days, setting new temperature records as extreme weather events become increasingly frequent across the continent.

## Technology & AI

### Enterprise AI Deployments Accelerate
- **KPMG** is deploying Anthropic's Claude to **276,000 employees** across 138 countries — one of the largest enterprise AI rollouts to date.
- **OpenAI** launched **DeployCo**, a $4 billion consulting subsidiary focused on enterprise AI deployment, signalling intensifying competition for who controls how AI reaches large organisations.

### Cohere Acquires Aleph Alpha
Canadian AI company Cohere acquired Germany's Aleph Alpha, forming a transatlantic sovereign AI firm valued at approximately **$20 billion** — a significant move toward European AI independence.

### AI Regulation Tightens
- Canada's Privacy Commissioner ruled that **OpenAI violated Canadian privacy laws** in the development of ChatGPT.
- The US government is pushing major AI labs — including Microsoft and xAI — to provide regulators with **early model access** before public release.

### Nemotron Coalition Launched
NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang joined leaders from Mistral, Perplexity, Cursor, Reflection AI, and others at GTC 2026 to announce the **Nemotron Coalition**, aiming to advance open frontier-level AI models through shared expertise, data, and compute.

### AI Energy Demand Hits Record
US power consumption is projected to hit record highs in 2026 and 2027, driven heavily by AI and cryptocurrency data centres. Demand is forecast to rise from 4,195 billion kWh in 2025 to 4,244 billion kWh in 2026.

### Breakthrough in AI Computing
Researchers at the University of Pennsylvania created a hybrid light-matter particle that could dramatically accelerate AI computing while significantly reducing energy consumption.
