## 🤖 AI & Technology

### Anthropic Claude Offline — Big Story
The US government's **June 12 export control directive** forced Anthropic to pull **Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 offline globally**. The API is still returning errors as of today (June 21). Anthropic's Chris Ciauri said restoration would happen "within days" on June 18 — but nothing yet. Traders are pricing ~58–67% odds of restoration before July 1.

### OpenAI Hits 1 Billion Monthly Active Users
ChatGPT surpassed the symbolic milestone of **1 billion monthly active users**. It still leads at 54.7% of worldwide AI chatbot web visits (down from 76.5% in Feb 2025), while **Google Gemini climbed to 27.4%** — up ~104% in six months.

### Gemini 3.5 Pro Still Not Public
Google CEO Sundar Pichai committed at Google I/O (May 19) to a June general availability window for Gemini 3.5 Pro — nine days left and it's still limited-preview for select Vertex AI enterprise customers only.

### OpenAI's New Memory Architecture
OpenAI launched its most significant memory upgrade since the original ChatGPT rollout — the **Dreaming V3 architecture** — now rolling out to ChatGPT Plus and Pro users in the US.

### AI Policy
- President Trump signed an **Executive Order on AI Innovation and Security** (June 2), positioning the US as the global AI leader while explicitly blocking mandatory government licensing of AI models.
- **Colorado's Consumer Protections for AI Act** takes effect June 30 — requiring risk management programs, annual impact assessments, and disclosure when AI drives consequential decisions.

### Infrastructure & Energy
FERC just reshaped how America powers AI infrastructure. **Microsoft added over 4 GW of new capacity** in the past 18 months; CoreWeave targets 1.7 GW by end of 2026. The grid cannot handle current build rates without further regulatory intervention.

### Other

- Agentic AI systems are the defining trend: AI moving from chat to task completion across research, coding, legal, payments, and commerce.

## 🇨🇿 Czech News

### Public Media Strike
A **one-day warning strike** is expected to disrupt programming across most Czech Television and Czech Radio channels (except children's broadcasts). Employees are protesting the government's planned overhaul of public media funding, arguing it would dismantle the current financing system and undermine editorial independence. Solidarity supporters are being asked to **wear black on Monday**.

### Skyfox Jets for Ukraine
PM Andrej Babiš is pushing European countries that supplied Ukraine with F-16s to help co-finance the purchase of **10 Czech L-39NG Skyfox training aircraft** for Ukraine. The deal is estimated at **CZK 5.3–6.3 billion**, with deliveries unlikely before 2028–2029.

### Weather Alert
Record heat is hitting Czechia, with **storms and flooding risks expected this weekend**.

### Rescue Story
A 74-year-old German tourist who went missing for two days in **Bohemian Switzerland** was found exhausted inside a cave near Dravčí skály after a large search operation involving police, firefighters, and park rangers.

## 🏀 NBA

### New York Knicks Win the 2026 NBA Championship!
The **New York Knicks defeated the San Antonio Spurs 4-1** in the 2026 NBA Finals (June 3–13) — their **first championship since 1973** (3rd overall). The series averaged **20+ million viewers** on ABC/ESPN — the most-watched NBA postseason since 1998.

- **Finals MVP: Jalen Brunson** — 45 points in Game 5, and 22 clutch points across the series (most in the Finals in 15 years).
- **Iconic moment:** Game 4 — Knicks erased a **29-point deficit** and won 107-106 on **OG Anunoby's last-second tip-in**.
- The Knicks rallied from double-digit deficits in all four of their victories.
