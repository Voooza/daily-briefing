President **Donald Trump** announced he plans to nominate acting Attorney General **Todd Blanche** to permanently lead the Department of Justice. Blanche has been serving in an acting capacity since former AG Pam Bondi was fired in April.

The Senate held a vote-a-rama as Republicans pushed to fund immigration agencies under the Department of Homeland Security without Democratic support. A move to ban the DOJ from receiving certain funds was rejected in the process.

### Israel–Lebanon Ceasefire Extended
