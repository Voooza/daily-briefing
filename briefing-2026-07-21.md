### Kimi K3 Overwhelms Moonshot's Compute, Rattles Chip Stocks

### EU Fines AliExpress a Record €550M Under the Digital Services Act
The European Commission handed **AliExpress** its largest-ever DSA penalty — €550 million — for failing to police counterfeit and unsafe products on its marketplace, citing understaffed brand-verification systems and inadequate risk assessment. The ruling lands days after Brussels also ordered Google to open Android to rival AI assistants, underscoring a busier-than-usual DSA/DMA enforcement stretch this month.
